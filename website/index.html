Playfirst Platform Beta nuts & bolts

Some of the finer details about the Playfirst platform.

Learning management

Game authors are able to manage exercises and learning paths for players, without having to play the game. They can focus on delivering quality content.

Analytics & tracking

Everything is tracked so that game authors are able to see how players are interacting with their content. We believe learning should be collaborative.

User roles & access rights

Not all users are equal - administrators are able to manage users and empower them to focus only on specific areas of the experience.

Extensions

Our thinking around 3rd party integrations carries over to extensions. Playfirst is straightforward and easily extended to cater for any need, whether it be new exercise types or a brand new feature altogether.

PLAY framework
"Secret formula"

PLAY is an architecture and component library that let's us customise the playing experience easily and quickly - including RPG, SIM or strategy style gameplay.

Multiplayer
Alpha

Every game is better when it's played with friends. PLAY includes online multiplayer capabilities that enables players inhabit the game world in real time with other players.

Traditional learning

Activities offer educators a mechanism to add traditional learning exercises to the game. These activities can include multiple choice questions, video, images or rich text.

Practical learning

Game authors are able to round off theoretical knowledge with mini-games, giving the learner a much better understanding of what they are learning.

Achievements

Reward players! Players are able to accumulate achievements for completing missions - which they can use for building their world or simply beating their friends.

Leaderboard

Players are able to keep track of how they're doing by viewing the Leaderboard. Some friendly competition can be great for incentivizing learning & content engagement.

Social

At it's heart Playfirst is a social learning experience. Players are able to "like" each other's units and also view a latest activity stream of other player interactions.

Missions & player paths

Game authors are able to group activities & exercises and create learning paths for players, known in-game as missions. Missions can have pre-requisites, forcing the player think about playing tactics.

Artificial Intelligence

Playfirst utilise artifically intelligent objects that enable the game to come to life without requiring anything from the player or game author.

The AI object library is currently being extended, companies will be notified as objects become available for use in-game.

     

Cross platform

Playfirst PLAY uses Unity3D™ as a base engine. This enables the game to be published to a wide variety of devices seamlessly. This include Windows™, MacOS™, iOS™ and Android™ among others.

Real time in-game chat

Empower users to collaborate with instructors & friends using real time in-game chat. Collaboration & communication is key.

Guided playing

Playfirst has an in-game assistant you can leverage to deliver hints & guides to players to not leave them stranded.

White labeling & customization

Leverage all of the functionality with completely different game world & user interface aesthetics. Playfirst can be customized to be unique to a brand as well as the learning material.
